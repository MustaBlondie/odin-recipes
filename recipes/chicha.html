Chicha

This is a dish from Venezuela

Venezuelan Chicha is a refreshing rice-based drink popular in Venezuela, sweet and creamy,often flavored with cinnamon and condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • cinnamon and condensed milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse 1 cup of rice and place it in a pot with 4 cups of water.
  2. Add 2 cinnamon sticks to the pot and bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  4. Remove the cinnamon sticks and let the rice mixture cool.
  5. Once cooled, blend the rice mixture in a blender until smooth.
  6. Return the blended mixture to the pot and add 1 cup of condensed milk and ½ cup of sugar.
  7. Stir well and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  9. Stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  10. Serve the chicha de arroz chilled and enjoy!
